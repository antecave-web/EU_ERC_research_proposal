# EU MSCA 2024 Research Grant Proposal

Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) 2024 research funding proposal developed for the European Union Horizon Europe programme.

Although the proposal was not funded through the MSCA call, it received a **Seal of Excellence**, indicating that it successfully passed the European Commission's quality threshold and was recommended for funding through alternative funding mechanisms.

This repository contains the full proposal and evaluation outcome.

---

## Project Overview

The Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions programme is among the European Union's most competitive research funding schemes, requiring applicants to demonstrate excellence across:

* Scientific quality
* Project feasibility
* Research impact
* Dissemination strategy
* Risk management
* Budget justification
* Ethics and regulatory compliance
* International collaboration

This proposal was developed as a complete research project plan in accordance with Horizon Europe requirements.

## Why This Project Matters

Large-scale funding proposals require many of the same competencies used in public administration, programme management, policy development, and organizational planning.

Producing a competitive MSCA proposal involves:

* Translating broad objectives into actionable plans
* Managing complex requirements and regulations
* Identifying risks and dependencies
* Coordinating multiple project components
* Demonstrating measurable outcomes and societal impact

These are transferable skills relevant far beyond academic research.

## Outcome

The proposal received a **Seal of Excellence** from the European Commission.

This distinction is awarded to proposals that successfully pass the Commission's quality assessment process and are considered worthy of funding, but cannot be supported within the available call budget.
